Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

To place an order, follow these steps:

  1. Browse our products and add items to your cart.
  2. Go to your cart and review the items.
  3. Click on the checkout button and provide your shipping and payment information.
  4. Review the order summary and confirm your purchase.
  5. You will receive a confirmation email with your order details.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfer

How long does shipping take?

The shipping time depends on your location and the shipping method selected. Typically, it takes 2-5 business days for domestic orders and 7-14 business days for international orders.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the item within 30 days of receiving it for a full refund. Please refer to our Returns page for more information.
